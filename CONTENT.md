## **Our Story**

Innovation ecosystems are filled with talented founders, experienced investors, valuable programs, and trusted service providers.

The problem is not a lack of opportunity.

The problem is that access to those opportunities is often fragmented.

Startups are expected to navigate disconnected networks, application portals, investor databases, email introductions, events, and data rooms. Investors receive opportunities from multiple sources without a consistent way to organize, compare, and manage them. Ecosystem organizations rely on combinations of spreadsheets, generic customer relationship systems, event platforms, and communication tools that were not built for startup investment communities.

Forjyn was created to solve this fragmentation.

We are building a connected platform where startups can prepare and present their companies, investors can discover opportunities aligned with their interests, and ecosystem partners can support both groups through stronger technology and shared infrastructure.

## **Our Mission**

### **Make Startup Growth Easier, Faster and More Connected**

Our mission is to connect startups, investors, ecosystem partners, and service providers through intelligent technology that improves access to capital, resources, knowledge, and opportunity.

## **Our Vision**

### **A Connected Global Innovation Ecosystem**

We envision a future where high-potential startups are not limited by geography, existing relationships, or access to traditional networks.

Forjyn aims to become the operating system through which innovation communities discover opportunities, build relationships, conduct due diligence, coordinate programs, and support company growth.

## **What We Believe**

### **Strong Companies Are Built Through Strong Ecosystems**

Capital is important, but startups also require knowledge, customers, talent, services, mentorship, infrastructure, and community.

Technology should make these resources easier to access.

### **Better Information Supports Better Decisions**

Founders need to understand what investors require. Investors need organized, credible information. Ecosystem partners need visibility into the communities they support.

Forjyn creates more structured information flows across the ecosystem.

### **Artificial Intelligence Should Support Human Judgment**

AI can organize information, highlight patterns, improve efficiency, and assist with evaluation.

Investment decisions, founder relationships, and company-building remain human responsibilities.

### **Trust Must Be Built Into the Product**

Users share sensitive identity, financial, company, and investment information.

Security, access control, auditability, and responsible data handling must be foundational—not optional additions.

### **Opportunity Should Not Depend Entirely on Existing Networks**

The best founders are not always the most connected founders.

Forjyn is designed to help investors discover promising companies beyond their immediate circles and help startups access stakeholders who may otherwise be difficult to reach.
